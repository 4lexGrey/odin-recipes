BLT Sandwich

Recipe

Bacon lettuce and tomato, it's in the name.

Tools

  • Toaster
  • Skillet
  • Spoon
  • Paper Towel

Ingredients

  • 2 Toasted Bread slices
  • 4 Bacon strips
  • 2 Lettuce Leaves
  • 2 Tomato slices
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Cook Bacon and let dry on paper towel
  3. Slice tomato and pull two lettuce leaves
  4. Spread mayo on one side of each slice of bread
  5. Arrange the tomato, bacon, and lettuce on one slice of bread
  6. Close Sandwich
