Peanut Butter & Jelly

Recipe

While it might seam obvious to us americans, other countries don't know about this common american delicacy.

Tools

  • Spoon
  • (optional) Toaster

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • (optional) Banana

Steps

  1. (optional) Toast bread
  2. Spread Jelly on one side of bread
  3. Wipe spoon clean
  4. Spread Peanut Butter on one side of the other slice of bread
  5. (optional) Lay banana slices on the peanut butter
  6. Close the sandwich by laying the bread jelly side down on the peanut butter slice
