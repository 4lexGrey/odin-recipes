Grilled Cheese

Recipe

Grilled Cheese is a classic sandwich that everyone should know.

Tools

  • Stove
  • Skillet
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pre Heat skillet
  2. Butter one side of bread and place face down on skillet
  3. Lay cheese on top
  4. Butter one side of other slice of bread and lay it face up on cheese
  5. Flip the sandwich and keep flipping till the cheese melts
